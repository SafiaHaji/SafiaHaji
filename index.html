Name of guest of honor :: Prof. Fortunata Songora Makene


 
     This 7th convocation of the eastern African Statistical Training Centre 
     of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of 
     gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactorss, and 
     industry professional as stakeholders of the university community.

     This year's convocation has selected the topic "The role of official 
     statistics and big data in the economic transformation and 
     sustainable development of Tanzania"as the standing point to 
     reflect on the theme of this year's 18 November African statistics
     Day. The theme states "Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the
     implementation of African Continental Free Area (AfCFTA): The role of  
     official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and 
     sustainable development of Africa
